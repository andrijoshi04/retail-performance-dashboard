# Retail Sales and Store Performance Analysis

## Problem Statement
Retail managers require a centralized view of sales performance across outlets, customer segments, and product collections to make informed business decisions and improve overall performance.

## Analysis Performed
An interactive Power BI dashboard was developed to analyze revenue trends, customer segmentation, and outlet performance. The analysis included:
* Revenue trends over time
* Sales distribution across customer segments (Women, Men, Children)
* Order volume by seasonal collections (Fall, Spring, Summer)
* Outlet performance based on customer ratings
* Comparison of actual performance against predefined targets

## Key Insights
* The Women segment contributes the highest share of total revenue
* The Fall collection generates the highest number of orders
* Certain outlets consistently outperform others in customer ratings
* There is a noticeable gap between actual and target order volumes

## Business Recommendations
* Increase marketing focus on the Women segment to maximize revenue
* Expand inventory and promotional efforts for the Fall collection
* Replicate strategies from high-performing outlets across other locations
* Implement targeted initiatives to improve order conversion and meet targets

## Tools Used
Power BI, Excel
